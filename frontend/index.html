🚗 While Travelling to the Vet
Keep your pet warm and as still as possible
Do not give food or water unless your vet instructs you to
Restrict movement if a fracture or spinal injury is suspected
Note the exact time symptoms started and describe them clearly
Bring any substances, plants, or objects your pet may have ingested
Have someone else drive so you can monitor your pet
Keep your pet's head elevated if they are vomiting
Use a blanket to prevent shock — even in warm weather
